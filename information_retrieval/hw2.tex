\begin{homeworkProblem}
  \begin{CJK}{UTF8}{bsmi} % 開始 CJK

    Exercise 6.12\\
    How does the base of the logarithm in (6.7) affect the score calculation in (6.9)? How\\
    does the base of the logarithm affect the relative scores of two documents on a given query?
    
    \textbf{Solution}

    the precise base of the logarithm has not effect to ranking, but rescale the score.\\
    $idf(b) = \log_b(\frac{N}{df}) = \frac{\log_{10}\frac{N}{df}}{\log_{10}b}$\\
    $\frac{idf(b)}{idf(10)}$ = $\frac{1}{b\log_{10}}$ is a constant\\
    So score will be divided by $\log_{10}(b)$, and the query result will not change

  \end{CJK} % 結束 CJK 環境 
\end{homeworkProblem}
